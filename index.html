About Us

The Lincoln Civic Center serves as a hub for community events, sports, and gatherings. Located in Brookhaven, MS, our center includes a multi-purpose building, rodeo arena, stall barn, 39-slot RV park, and five little league baseball fields.

Our Facilities

  • Multi-Purpose Building
  • Rodeo Arena
  • Stall Barn
  • 39-Slot RV Park
  • 5 Little League Baseball Parks
