
To mark a site as "Rented":

1. Open map.svg in a text editor.
2. Find the site by ID, for example:
   <rect id="site-12" ... />
3. Add class="rented" like this:
   <rect id="site-12" class="rented" ... />

This will visually show the site as rented (red overlay).
